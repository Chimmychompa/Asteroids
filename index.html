Asteroids

Objective:
- Avoid colliding with asteroids, shoot them instead ;)
But watch out! Asteroids may split up if they are too massive!!

Controls:
- Left/Right arrow keys to turn.
- Up arrow key to boost.
- Spacebar to shoot.
- When you lose a life, you turn GOLDEN, meaning that
you are invincible for a short amount of time.
use this time wisely!
- F to teleport to a random spot, this also turns you GOLDEN (Long cooldown)

Good Luck!
